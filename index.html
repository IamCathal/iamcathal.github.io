Studyclix Userbase Analysis

I did an analysis of Studyclix.ie to see whether their user base of students is an accurate representation of students currently in 3rd year to 6th year around the country. I compared the amount of students doing each subject to the official statistics released by the SEC. This was my first project involving any kind of 'data science' and I thoroughly enjoyed working through it. Click the image on the left to view.
